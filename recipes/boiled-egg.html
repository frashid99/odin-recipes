Boiled Egg

Description

This is a guide on how to make a boiled egg. If you don't know what a boiled egg is already then I cant help you.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 4 cups of water
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Pour water into a pot and boil
  2. Place your egg in the pot as well
  3. Wait 20 minutes for the egg to boil
  4. Now take the pot off the stove and let the egg cool down
  5. Once cooled, crack open your boiled egg
  6. Now slice in half and top off with some mayonnaise if you desire
  7. Enjoy!
